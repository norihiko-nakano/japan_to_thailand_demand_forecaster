# Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Title
Japan-to-Thailand Travel Demand Direction Forecaster  
Inspired by Moo Deng 🦛

## Slide 2: Business Problem
Japanese travel demand changes by holidays, seasonality, exchange rates, and external shocks.

## Slide 3: Final Vision
Predict which destinations or tours should be promoted on Japanese-language travel pages.

## Slide 4: MVP Scope
Predict whether Japanese visitors to Thailand will increase or decrease next month.

## Slide 5: Data Limitation
Public data does not reliably show where Japanese travelers go inside Thailand.

## Slide 6: MVP Data
Monthly Japanese visitors to Thailand, lag features, seasonality, holiday indicators, exchange-rate proxy, shock flag.

## Slide 7: Shock Handling
COVID-era months are flagged and modeled transparently.

## Slide 8: Model
Baseline, Logistic Regression, Random Forest.

## Slide 9: Business Output
Demand likely to increase / decrease and recommended page promotion action.

## Slide 10: Future Work
News, X, TV exposure, Google Trends, internal search/click/booking data, destination-level demand.
