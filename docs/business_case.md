# Business Case

## Problem

Japanese travelers do not behave uniformly throughout the year. Demand changes around Golden Week, Obon, New Year holidays, exchange rates, airline prices, external shocks, and viral travel buzz.

## MVP Business Question

Will Japanese demand for Thailand increase next month?

## Business Action

If demand is likely to increase, a travel platform can promote Thailand on Japanese-language pages, highlight Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai, and Pattaya, and prepare campaign messaging around holidays.

If demand is likely to decrease, the platform can reduce campaign exposure, promote alternative destinations, or wait for stronger demand signals.

## Long-Term Vision

With internal platform data, the model can become destination-level: Bangkok hotels, Phuket beach resorts, Chiang Mai seasonal trips, Pattaya / Chonburi day trips, and Khao Kheow Open Zoo tours inspired by Moo Deng.
